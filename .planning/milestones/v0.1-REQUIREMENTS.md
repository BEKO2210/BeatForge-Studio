## Overview

Requirements for a browser-based music-to-video visualizer creator. Beat-reactive visuals are the core differentiator.

## v1 Requirements

### Audio (AUD)

| ID | Requirement | Priority | Status |
|----|-------------|----------|--------|
| AUD-01 | User can upload MP3/WAV audio files | Must | Complete |
| AUD-02 | System performs real-time beat detection | Must | Complete |
| AUD-03 | System performs real-time frequency analysis | Must | Complete |
| AUD-04 | Audio playback with transport controls (play/pause/seek) | Must | Complete |

## Milestone Summary

**Shipped:** 35 of 38 v1 requirements

**Adjusted during implementation:**
- TXT-04: Karaoke sync simplified to animation only (no timeline-based lyrics)
- FX-03: Post-processing limited to vignette (bloom/chromatic aberration deferred)

**Dropped:**
- VIS-04: Particle effects (implemented but reverted due to performance concerns)

**Deferred (Could priority):**
- BG-04: Video backgrounds
- FX-04: Color grading
- PRV-03: Performance mode
- EXP-02: MP4 export (browser WebCodecs support inconsistent)
